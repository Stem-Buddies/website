To understand Cecilia's discovery, we need to do a little experiment ourselves. Here's what you'll need:

ğŸ” **What's Happening?** Light is made up of a rainbow of different colours (wavelengths). When we see a red light, it means that source is giving off (emitting) mostly red wavelengths. The coloured filters work by only letting through the wavelengths that match their colour.
